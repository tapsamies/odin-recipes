Lasagna

Description

Fast. Easy. Cheap. There really isn't much to say about lasagna is there, anyways you aren't here to read about lasagna, so let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tsp of salt, pepper and cumin
  • 1 spaghetti sauce jar
  • 1 pound of cheese

Steps

  1. Boil 2 litres of salted water and add pasta. Cook for 10 minutes and drain afterwards
  2. Heat oven to 175 celcius, while heating brown beef and season. Add pasta sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Shred cheese, mix it with beef and add to pan. Alternate layers between pasta and meat
  4. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
