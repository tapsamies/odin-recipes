Macaroni and cheese

Description

Fast. Easy. Cheap. There really isn't much to say about Macaroni and cheese is there, anyways you aren't here to read about Macaroni and cheese, so let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of Macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Boil 2 litres of salted water and add Macaroni. Cook for 10 minutes and drain afterwards
  2. Melt butter, add flour. After 5 minutes pour milk slowly to mix, add cheese and stir until melted
  3. Fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated
